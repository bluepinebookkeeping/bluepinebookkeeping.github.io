About Blue Pine Bookkeeping

Welcome to Blue Pine Bookkeeping! My name is Stephen Pierce, and I love to help other small businesses with their financial needs. With over 10 years of experience in bookkeeping, I have the expertise and knowledge to provide the highest quality services to my clients.

My goal is to make bookkeeping as stress-free and straightforward as possible, so you can focus on running your business.

Experience and Skills

Prior to starting Blue Pine Bookkeeping, I worked in a variety of industries, including construction, manufacturing, and retail. This experience has given me a broad perspective on the challenges facing small businesses and has helped me develop the skills necessary to provide effective bookkeeping solutions.

I'm a certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor and have extensive experience working with QuickBooks Online and Desktop, as well as other accounting software. I'm also proficient in Design Manager and Studio Designer, two popular software programs used in the interior design industry.

If you have any questions or would like to learn more about my services, please don't hesitate to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
